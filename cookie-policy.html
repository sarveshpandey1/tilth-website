Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This page explains how cookies are (and aren't) used on tilth.in.

As of today, tilth.in does not set any cookies of its own, and does not use analytics or advertising trackers.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files a website can store in your browser, often used to remember preferences or track behaviour across visits. Many sites use them heavily. This one does not.

Cookies we use

None set by us. We don't use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any similar tracking technology on this site at this time.

Third-party connections

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts. This involves a connection to Google's servers but does not, by itself, set advertising cookies on your device. If you click through to our social profiles (LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Facebook), those platforms will apply their own cookies once you are on their sites.

Managing cookies

Because we don't set any, there's nothing here for you to opt out of. If you'd like to control cookies generally, every major browser lets you view and delete them in its settings.

If this changes

If we later add analytics or other tools that use cookies, we will update this page first, add a clear notice, and provide consent options where required by law.
