Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This policy explains what happens to your information when you visit tilth.in. We have deliberately kept this site simple, and the same goes for how we handle data.

The short version

We don't run analytics, we don't set our own cookies, and we don't have any forms that collect or store your information. The only way to contact us through this site is by email, which you choose to send.

Information we collect

Through this website, Tilth does not actively collect, store, or process personal information. There is no contact form, no account system, and no newsletter signup on the site.

If you email us at anuja@tilth.in, we receive whatever information you choose to include in that email (such as your name, email address, and message). We use it only to reply to you and discuss working together.

Google Fonts

This site uses Google Fonts to display its typefaces. When the fonts load, your browser connects to Google's servers, and in doing so your IP address is visible to Google. This is standard for most websites using Google Fonts, but we want to be transparent that it happens. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may collect standard technical information (such as IP addresses) in server logs for security and operational purposes. This is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

External links

Our site links to external profiles (LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Facebook). Once you follow those links, you are subject to the privacy policies of those platforms, not ours.

Your rights

Since we don't store personal data through this site, there is little for us to hold or delete. If you have emailed us and would like us to delete that correspondence, just ask at anuja@tilth.in and we will.

Changes to this policy

As Tilth grows, we may add tools such as analytics, a contact form, or a CRM. If and when we do, we will update this policy to reflect exactly what is collected and why, and change the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email anuja@tilth.in.
