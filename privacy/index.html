Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This policy explains what happens to your information when you visit tilth.in. We have deliberately kept this site simple, and the same goes for how we handle data.

The short version

We keep data collection to a minimum. We use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used, and we offer a contact form so you can request a free audit. We don't sell your data, we don't run advertising pixels, and we don't send marketing emails you didn't ask for.

Information we collect

Contact form. If you submit the form on our contact page, we collect the details you provide — your name, email address, brand or website, the channels you select, and your message. We use this only to reply to you and discuss working together. The form is delivered to us by FormSubmit, a third-party form-to-email service; its handling of the submission is governed by the FormSubmit service.

Email. If you email us at anuja@tilth.in, we receive whatever information you choose to include in that email. We use it only to reply and discuss working together.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site — which pages are viewed, the device and browser used, approximate location (city or country, derived from IP), and how visitors arrived. This is collected via cookies and analysed in aggregate to help us improve the site; we do not use it to identify you personally. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Fonts

This site uses Google Fonts to display its typefaces. When the fonts load, your browser connects to Google's servers, and in doing so your IP address is visible to Google. This is standard for most websites using Google Fonts, but we want to be transparent that it happens. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may collect standard technical information (such as IP addresses) in server logs for security and operational purposes. This is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

External links

Our site links to external profiles (LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Facebook). Once you follow those links, you are subject to the privacy policies of those platforms, not ours.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete any personal information you've sent us — a form submission or email correspondence — at any time. Just email anuja@tilth.in and we will. You can also opt out of analytics using the browser add-on linked above.

Changes to this policy

As Tilth grows, we may add or change the tools we use. If and when we do, we will update this policy to reflect exactly what is collected and why, and change the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email anuja@tilth.in.
