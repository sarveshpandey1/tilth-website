Cookie Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This page explains how cookies are used on tilth.in.

tilth.in uses Google Analytics, which sets a small number of cookies to measure how the site is used. We do not use advertising or social-media tracking cookies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files a website can store in your browser, often used to remember preferences or measure how a site is used. Many sites use them heavily. We use only a few, for analytics.

Cookies we use

Google Analytics (GA4). We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. It sets first-party cookies (typically named _ga and _ga_*) that help distinguish unique visitors and measure usage in aggregate. These cookies don't identify you personally and we don't use them for advertising. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use the Meta Pixel, advertising cookies, or cross-site tracking on this site.

Third-party connections

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts. This involves a connection to Google's servers but does not, by itself, set advertising cookies on your device. If you click through to our social profiles (LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Facebook), those platforms will apply their own cookies once you are on their sites.

Managing cookies

Every major browser lets you view, block, and delete cookies in its settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics specifically using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

If this changes

If we later add other tools that use cookies, we will update this page first and provide consent options where required by law.
